AR shopping is the future

But a lot needs to be done to get there

  • To enable AR experiences, retailers need a 3D map of the store's products

  • However, this information is currently distributed between planograms, CAD plans, and people's heads

  • Planograms change on a weekly basis, so manual data entry will be too expensive

  • Our software reads planograms and creates a 3D product map
